<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Semiconductor Lithography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>KrF</li><li>I-Line</li><li>ArF Dry</li><li>EUV</li><li>ArF Immersion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor lithography equipment includes various technologies for patterning integrated circuits. KrF (Krypton Fluoride) and ArF (Argon Fluoride) dry lithography use ultraviolet light for creating circuit features, with KrF offering a larger wavelength and lower resolution compared to ArF. I-Line lithography, utilizing wavelengths around 365 nm, is suited for less advanced processes. ArF immersion technology enhances resolution by immersing the wafer in water during exposure, while EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) lithography utilizes shorter wavelengths for cutting-edge manufacturing, enabling smaller feature sizes.</p></p>
