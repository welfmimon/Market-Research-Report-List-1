<p><p>The Irrigation Supervisory Controller market comprises two main types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data to optimize irrigation schedules, adjusting water application based on rainfall, temperature, and humidity. In contrast, sensor-based controllers incorporate soil moisture sensors, detecting the water needs of plants directly. Both types aim to enhance water efficiency, reduce waste, and improve crop health, catering to diverse agricultural practices while promoting sustainable irrigation management solutions.</p></p>
