<p><p>The commercial vehicle telematics market encompasses two primary types: hardware equipment and software systems. Software systems, on the other hand, involve applications and platforms that analyze and interpret this data, offering insights into vehicle performance, driver behavior, and fleet management. Together, these components enhance operational efficiency, improve safety, and reduce operational costs for commercial fleets.</p></p>
