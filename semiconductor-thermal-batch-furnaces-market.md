<p><p>The semiconductor thermal batch furnaces market is primarily divided into two types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal batch furnaces feature a design where wafers are loaded horizontally, facilitating easier handling and uniform heat distribution, making them suitable for larger production volumes. In contrast, vertical batch furnaces utilize a stacked arrangement, optimizing space and enhancing thermal efficiency, which is beneficial for high-temperature processes. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing, impacting yield and performance in various applications.</p></p>
