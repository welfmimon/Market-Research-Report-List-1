<p><p>The Split Tray Sorter Market comprises two primary types: Single Split and Double Split. In Single Split systems, trays are divided into two sections, allowing for efficient sorting of items into two distinct categories simultaneously. Conversely, Double Split systems feature trays that can accommodate multiple splits, enabling the sorting of items into several categories at once. Both types enhance operational efficiency in logistics and distribution centers, optimizing space and streamlining sorting processes for varied applications in different industries.</p></p>
