<p><p>The common mode chip inductors market is categorized into two primary types: through-hole type and surface-mount device (SMD) type. Through-hole inductors are designed for traditional circuit boards, offering robust connections and high power handling, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, SMD inductors are compact, enabling high-density assembly on printed circuit boards, which is ideal for modern electronics. Each type has distinct advantages, catering to different application requirements in industries like telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
