<p><p>The wearable biological sensor market comprises various types, including epidermal biosensors, eye biosensors, and dental biosensors. Epidermal biosensors are designed for continuous monitoring of health indicators via the skin, utilizing flexible materials for comfort. Eye biosensors focus on analyzing ocular fluids to gather information about systemic health, offering non-invasive diagnostics. Dental biosensors monitor oral health by detecting changes in saliva or gum conditions, aiding in the prevention and management of dental diseases, and enhancing overall well-being.</p></p>
