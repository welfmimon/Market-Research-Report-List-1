<p><strong>The Automatic Laser Plotter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Drum Laser Photoplotters</li><li>Internal Drum Laser Photoplotters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic laser plotter market is primarily divided into two types: external drum and internal drum laser photoplotters. External drum laser photoplotters utilize an external rotating drum to hold the substrate, allowing for larger formats and versatility in media types. In contrast, internal drum laser photoplotters feature a drum that contains the laser mechanism within, offering precision and compact design, ideal for detailed and high-resolution imaging. Both types cater to the evolving needs of industries requiring advanced printing technology.</p></p>
