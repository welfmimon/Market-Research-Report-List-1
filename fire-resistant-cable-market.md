<p><p>The fire-resistant cable market includes various types designed for specific applications. LSOH (Low Smoke Zero Halogen) cables emit minimal smoke and no halogen when burned, enhancing safety in enclosed spaces. UL Series cables meet stringent Underwriters Laboratories standards for fire performance. CMP (Communications Multipurpose Plenum) cables are rated for air-handling spaces, while CMR (Communications Multipurpose Risers) are suited for vertical installations. CM (Communications Multipurpose) cables are suitable for general use, and CMG (General Purpose) cables and CMX (General Purpose for outdoor use) offer versatile applications in low-risk environments.</p></p>
