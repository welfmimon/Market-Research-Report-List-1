<p><p>Horticultural maintenance services encompass two main market types: corrective maintenance and preventive maintenance. Corrective maintenance addresses issues after they arise, focusing on the repair and restoration of plants and landscapes to optimal health following damage or decline. In contrast, preventive maintenance emphasizes ongoing care and practices designed to avert potential problems, including regular plant inspections, fertilization, and pruning. Together, these services ensure the longevity and beauty of outdoor spaces, catering to both immediate needs and long-term sustainability.</p></p>
