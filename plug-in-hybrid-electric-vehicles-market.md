<p><ul><li>Stored Electricity</li><li>On Board Electric Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) can be categorized into two primary market types: Stored Electricity and On-Board Electric Generator. Stored Electricity PHEVs utilize a rechargeable battery to store electric energy, allowing for electric-only driving for short distances before transitioning to a gasoline engine for extended range. In contrast, On-Board Electric Generator PHEVs rely on an internal combustion engine to generate electricity, which powers the electric motor, facilitating longer journeys without the need for frequent charging. Together, these types enhance efficiency and versatility in the automotive market.</p></p>
