<p><strong>The Polyimide Plastic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting</li><li>Thermoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide plastic market is segmented into two main types: thermosetting and thermoplastic. Thermosetting polyimides are rigid and heat-resistant, making them suitable for high-temperature applications like electronics and aerospace. Once cured, they cannot be remolded. In contrast, thermoplastic polyimides can be easily reshaped upon heating, offering flexibility for manufacturing processes. They are often used in applications requiring lightweight materials with good thermal and chemical stability, catering to diverse industries such as automotive, electronics, and telecommunications.</p></p>
