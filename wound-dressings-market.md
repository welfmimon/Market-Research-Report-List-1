<p><p>The wound dressings market comprises various types designed to manage and promote healing in different wound environments. Film dressings provide a protective barrier while allowing moisture vapor exchange. Foam dressings absorb excess exudate and cushion the wound. Collagen dressings facilitate cellular growth and tissue regeneration. Alginates, derived from seaweed, are highly absorbent and suitable for exuding wounds. Hydrocolloids form a gel upon contact with moisture, promoting a moist environment. Hydrogels maintain hydration, and super absorbers manage high levels of exudate effectively.</p></p>
