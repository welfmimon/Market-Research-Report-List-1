<p><ul><li>Active Safety Needles</li><li>Passive Safety Needles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hypodermic safety needles market is categorized into active and passive safety needles. Active safety needles require the user to manually activate a safety mechanism to retract the needle after use, enhancing protection against needlestick injuries. In contrast, passive safety needles automatically engage a safety feature once the needle is withdrawn, providing seamless protection without requiring additional user action. Both types aim to improve safety for healthcare workers while minimizing exposure to potential hazards associated with needle handling.</p></p>
