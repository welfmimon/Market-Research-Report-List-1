<p><strong>The Bone Harvester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cancellous Bone Harvesting</li><li>Marrow Harvesting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bone Harvester market consists primarily of two types: cancellous bone harvesting and marrow harvesting. Cancellous bone harvesting focuses on obtaining spongy bone tissue, which is crucial for orthopedic and reconstructive surgeries due to its high vascularity and ability to promote healing. Marrow harvesting, on the other hand, involves extracting hematopoietic bone marrow, vital for procedures like stem cell transplants and treating blood disorders. Both methods significantly enhance surgical outcomes by providing essential biological materials for various medical applications.</p></p>
