<p><p>The Long Range Radar Sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Long Range Millimeter Wave Radar Sensors and Long Range Laser Radar Sensors. Millimeter Wave Radar Sensors operate using electromagnetic waves, offering robust performance in various weather conditions for applications like automotive and security. In contrast, Long Range Laser Radar Sensors utilize laser light to achieve high-resolution imaging and accurate distance measurement, making them suitable for autonomous vehicles and advanced robotics. Both types enhance situational awareness and safety in their respective applications.</p></p>
