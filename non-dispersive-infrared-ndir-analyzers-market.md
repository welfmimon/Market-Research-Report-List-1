<p><p>Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) analyzers come in two main market types: single-channel and multi-channel. Single-channel NDIR analyzers are designed to measure one specific gas concentration at a time, making them ideal for applications requiring focused analysis. In contrast, multi-channel NDIR analyzers can simultaneously detect multiple gases, providing a broader analysis and higher efficiency for complex environments. Both types leverage infrared absorption principles, but their choice depends on the analytical needs and complexity of the gases involved.</p></p>
