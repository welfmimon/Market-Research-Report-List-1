<p><p>The copolymer alloys market can be categorized into two primary types based on the number of monomers used: two monomers and multiple monomers. </p><p>Two monomer copolymer alloys are formed by combining two distinct monomers, resulting in polymers with enhanced properties tailored for specific applications. Both types find applications in industries like automotive, packaging, and electronics.</p></p>
