<p><ul><li>1:1 Type Metal Complex Dye</li><li>1:2 Type Metal Complex Dye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acid Mordant Dyes market comprises various types, primarily categorized into 1:1 and 1:2 Type Metal Complex Dyes. 1:1 Type Metal Complex Dyes feature a single metal ion complexed with the dye, yielding vibrant hues and enhanced stability on textiles. In contrast, 1:2 Type Metal Complex Dyes involve two dye molecules interacting with one metal ion, resulting in deeper shades and improved lightfastness. Both types are widely used in dyeing wool, silk, and nylon due to their excellent color properties and affinity for protein fibers.</p></p>
