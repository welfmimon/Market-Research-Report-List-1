<p><p>The Seismic Perimeter Intrusion Detection System (PID) market comprises three main types: Seismic Sensors, Seismic Servers, and Seismic Receivers. Seismic Sensors detect ground vibrations caused by intrusions, providing primary data for security assessments. Seismic Servers process this data, analyzing signals to differentiate between genuine threats and environmental noise. Seismic Receivers receive and interpret the processed data, delivering alerts and information to security personnel. Together, these components create an effective layered defense against unauthorized access in secure environments.</p></p>
