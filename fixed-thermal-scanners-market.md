<p><strong>The Fixed Thermal Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed thermal scanners market is classified into three main types based on infrared wavelength: Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR scanners excel in low-light conditions and detect water content; MWIR devices are ideal for gas detection and can operate in harsh environments; LWIR scanners are widely used for temperature measurement and surveillance, as they are highly effective in thermal imaging applications, making them suitable for various industrial and security uses.</p></p>
