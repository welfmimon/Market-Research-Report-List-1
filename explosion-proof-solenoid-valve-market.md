<p><p>The explosion-proof solenoid valve market includes three main types: Two-Way, Three-Way, and Four-Way solenoid valves. Two-Way solenoid valves control the flow of media in a single direction, providing on/off functionality. Three-Way solenoid valves can divert or mix flow between two paths, serving diverse applications. Four-Way solenoid valves typically manage actuators or cylinders, controlling the flow in multiple directions. Each type is designed for use in hazardous environments, ensuring safety and reliability in critical applications.</p></p>
