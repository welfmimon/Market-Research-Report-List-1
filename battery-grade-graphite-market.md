<p><p>The battery-grade graphite market comprises three main types: crystalline flake graphite, amorphous graphite, and vein graphite (lump). Crystalline flake graphite is highly sought after due to its purity and conductive properties, making it ideal for lithium-ion batteries. Amorphous graphite is more abundant and less expensive, commonly used in various applications, including batteries. Vein graphite, or lump graphite, is rarer and exhibits high purity and unique properties, suitable for specialized battery technologies, contributing to efficient energy storage solutions.</p></p>
