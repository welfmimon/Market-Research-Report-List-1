<p><p>The antivenom immunoglobulin market consists of two primary types: polyvalent and monovalent antivenom. Polyvalent antivenom is designed to treat envenomations from multiple snake species, making it versatile in regions with diverse snake populations. In contrast, monovalent antivenom targets a specific snake species, offering a more tailored treatment for individual bites but limited application. The choice between these types depends on the geographical prevalence of snake species and the specific needs of patients responding to envenomation.</p></p>
