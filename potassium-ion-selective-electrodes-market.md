<p><p>The potassium ion selective electrodes (ISEs) market is primarily categorized into liquid membrane and PVC membrane types. Liquid membrane ISEs utilize a hydrophobic organic solvent that contains potassium ion-selective compounds, offering high sensitivity and fast response times. In contrast, PVC membrane ISEs are made from polyvinyl chloride mixed with potassium ion-selective materials, providing stability and durability. Both types are widely used in environmental monitoring, food quality control, and clinical diagnostics, catering to various analytical needs and applications.</p></p>
