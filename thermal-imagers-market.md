<p><p>The thermal imager market is primarily segmented into two types based on detector technologies: VOx (Vanadium Oxide) and a-Si (Amorphous Silicon). VOx detectors are known for their high sensitivity and performance in detecting minute temperature variations, making them ideal for military and industrial applications. In contrast, a-Si detectors are cost-effective and suitable for commercial use, though they generally offer lower sensitivity. This segmentation helps address varying customer needs across diverse industries, from security to maintenance and diagnostics.</p></p>
