The On-Axis Drive Module integrates the electric motor directly with the vehicle's axle, enhancing efficiency and responsiveness. In contrast, the Off-Axis Drive Module positions the motor away from the axle, offering design flexibility and potential space savings. Both types are integral in improving vehicle performance and efficiency in hybrid and electric powertrains.</p></p>
