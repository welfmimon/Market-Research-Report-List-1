<p><strong>The Pelvic Plating System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Locking</li><li>Non-Locking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pelvic Plating System market is divided into two main types: locking and non-locking plates. Locking plates provide stable fixation by creating a secure connection between the plate and screws, enhancing stability and enabling early mobility. They are often used in complex fractures. Non-locking plates, on the other hand, rely on traditional screw fixation and are suitable for simpler fractures. The choice between these systems depends on fracture type, patient needs, and surgeon preference, influencing overall surgical outcomes.</p></p>
