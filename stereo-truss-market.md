<p><strong>The Stereo Truss Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tetrahedron</li><li>Polyhedron</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stereo truss market is primarily divided into two types: tetrahedron and polyhedron. Tetrahedron trusses feature a triangular arrangement, offering excellent strength-to-weight ratios and stability, making them ideal for lightweight structures. In contrast, polyhedron trusses consist of multiple triangular faces, providing greater versatility and design options. These structures are often used in various applications, including event staging, architectural installations, and temporary structures, allowing for innovative designs while ensuring structural integrity and ease of assembly.</p></p>
