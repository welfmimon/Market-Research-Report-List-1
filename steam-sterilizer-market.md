<p><strong>The Steam Sterilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity</li><li>SFPP</li><li>Pre-Vac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam sterilizer market is categorized into three primary types: Gravity Displacement, Steam Flush Pressure Pulse (SFPP), and Pre-Vacuum. </p><p>Gravity Displacement sterilizers rely on steam rising and displacing air, suitable for simple applications. SFPP combines steam flush and pressure pulses to effectively remove air, enhancing penetration and sterilization efficiency for complex loads. Pre-Vacuum sterilizers utilize vacuum technology to eliminate air before steam is introduced, ensuring thorough sterilization, especially for porous materials and instruments. Each type caters to different sterilization needs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
