<p><p>Extreme Ultraviolet Lithography (EUVL) systems utilize different techniques to generate the extreme ultraviolet light essential for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Key market types include Laser Produced Plasmas (LPP), which use high-energy lasers to create plasma that emits EUV light; Vacuum Sparks, employing electric discharges in vacuum to produce high-intensity light; and Gas Discharges, which utilize ionized gas for EUV generation. Each method offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, scalability, and intensity, impacting their adoption in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
