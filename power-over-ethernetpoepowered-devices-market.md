<p><ul><li>Powered Device Controllers</li><li>Powered Device Ics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) powered devices market includes various components that enable the delivery of both power and data over standard Ethernet cables. Powered Device Controllers manage power distribution and optimize efficiency in network settings. They facilitate proper voltage and current management for PoE devices. In contrast, Powered Device Integrated Circuits (ICs) are semiconductor components that convert incoming electrical signals into usable power for networked devices. These technologies are crucial for enhancing connectivity and streamlining energy consumption in modern networking solutions.</p></p>
