<p><p>The Eternal Cavity Diode Laser (ECDL) market primarily features two types: Tunable External-Cavity Diode Lasers and Mode-Locked External-Cavity Diode Lasers. Tunable ECDLs allow for adjustable wavelengths, making them suitable for applications in spectroscopy and telecommunications. In contrast, Mode-Locked ECDLs generate short, pulse-like outputs, ideal for high-frequency applications such as optical communication and precision measurements. Both types emphasize flexibility and performance, catering to various sectors including research, medical, and industrial applications where precise control of laser properties is essential.</p></p>
