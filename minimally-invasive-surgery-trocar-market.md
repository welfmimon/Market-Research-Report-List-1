<p><p>The Minimally Invasive Surgery Trocar market comprises two primary types: disposable trocars and reposable trocars. Disposable trocars are single-use devices designed for one-time procedures, offering enhanced sterility and reduced infection risk. In contrast, reposable trocars are designed for multiple uses, featuring durable materials that can withstand several sterilization cycles, making them cost-effective over time. Both types serve critical roles in various surgical procedures, enabling precise access to internal cavities while minimizing trauma to surrounding tissues.</p></p>
