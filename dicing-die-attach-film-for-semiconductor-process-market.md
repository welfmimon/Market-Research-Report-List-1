<p><p>Dicing Die Attach Film is essential in the semiconductor industry for attaching individual dies to substrates. There are two main types in the market: Non-Conductive Type and Conductive Type. Non-Conductive Type films are primarily used for applications requiring electrical isolation, serving as a reliable adhesive layer. In contrast, Conductive Type films facilitate electrical connectivity, making them ideal for high-performance applications where electrical conductivity between the die and substrate is critical. Both types cater to specific needs within semiconductor packaging processes.</p></p>
