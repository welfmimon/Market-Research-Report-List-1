Vertical chain diaphragms are designed for more complex systems, often found in mechanical devices that require vertical movement. Corrugated diaphragms, with their distinct wavy structure, are effective in accommodating flex and pressure variations, making them ideal for dynamic applications. Each type is tailored to specific operational requirements, enhancing performance and reliability.</p></p>
