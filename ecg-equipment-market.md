<p><ul><li>Monitoring Equipment</li><li>Holter Monitoring Systems</li><li>Event Monitoring Systems</li><li>Stress Testing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECG equipment market encompasses various types, including monitoring equipment, Holter monitoring systems, event monitoring systems, and stress testing systems. Monitoring equipment is used for continuous observation of heart activity, while Holter monitoring systems provide portable, long-term data collection for diagnosing arrhythmias. Event monitoring systems enable patients to record specific ECG events, aiding in sporadic condition detection. Stress testing systems assess heart performance under physical exertion, providing crucial insights into cardiovascular health and potential abnormalities during stress conditions.</p></p>
